CONTROL / TELEMETRY
  UDP port ........... 7099   (20 Hz loop)
  Drone telemetry .... 53 01 00 00 00   (byte0=83 = model id, TC variant)
  Heartbeat (app) .... 01 01   every 1000 ms
  Camera switch ...... 06 01 (front) / 06 02 (rear)
  Photo/video sync ... 09 01 / 09 02
  Leave control ...... 08 01

  TC FLIGHT PACKET (9 bytes), sticks centered at 128 (min 1, max 255):
    03 66 B1 B2 ACC TURN FLAGS CKSUM 99
      B1=roll B2=pitch ACC=throttle TURN=yaw
      CKSUM = B1^B2^ACC^TURN^FLAGS
    FLAGS bits: 01 up/takeoff  02 down/land  04 e-stop  08 flip
                10 headless    20 return/unlock         80 gyro-calib
  WARNING: these spin the motors. Fly only in a safe open area.

*** CALIBRATE THE GYRO FIRST ***
  Before EVERY session: drone flat & still on the ground, press the amber
  "GYRO CALIBRATE" button (or key C). An uncalibrated gyro is the usual reason
  a drone drifts/tips/flips -- not the controls.

  python3 ufo.py connect --ssid WIFI-UFO-XXXXXX   connect to a specific drone
  python3 webapp.py           cockpit -> http://localhost:8088

RELIABILITY NOTES
  - .100 is PINNED to the wifi profile (survives reconnect).
  - Cockpit self-heals .100 if it drops.
  - Drone AUTO-POWERS-OFF when idle/low battery -> power-cycle + reconnect.
  - Physical remote: OK together with wifi video/telemetry; do NOT fly sticks
    from both remote and laptop at the same time (they fight).

COCKPIT KEYS (webapp.py — matches deepseek_pro)
  W/S = pitch   A/D = roll   Up/Dn = throttle   Left/Right = yaw
  SPACE = takeoff/land toggle   BACKSPACE = EMERGENCY STOP
  C = CALIBRATE GYRO   H = headless   F = flip
  Buttons add: GYRO CALIBRATE, ARM/DISARM, LIGHT (LED 0x40), CAM1/CAM2, SNAP,
  REC, ROTATE, ZOOM.  Rate: 1 Slow / 2 Normal / 3 Fast (= remote 1/2/3 beeps).
  Control model = deepseek_pro: continuous 20Hz stream, takeoff pulses throttle
  150 then auto-hovers, full 0-255 authority, + keyup auto-center safety.

CAMERAS
  ONE big live feed, rotated 90 deg by default (mount is sideways). Switch which
  camera feeds it with CAM1/CAM2 (06 01 / 06 02). ONE encoder -> only one camera
  at a time; true side-by-side is impossible. Switching re-inits the encoder so
  the stream briefly drops and the webapp auto-reconnects. (On some units CAM2
  shows the same front feed -> effectively one usable camera.)

ELECTRONICS (research)
  Chip: Jieli BL60x-class WiFi-camera SoC  (NOT an ESP32).
  Separate flight MCU holds the gyro/accelerometer (MPU6050-class) — its data
  never crosses to the WiFi chip.  See reference/electronics.md.

BATTERY / IMU TELEMETRY: NOT AVAILABLE over WiFi (confirmed by live probe:
  UDP opcode sweep 0x00-0xFF + HTTP + FTP -> drone only sends 53 01 00 00 00
  and 01 01 00 00 00). Cockpit shows battery as a flight-time ESTIMATE only.
