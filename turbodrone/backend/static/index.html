How To Fly

1. Takeoff
Press Space or click Takeoff.
Drone rises to ~1.5m and hovers.

2. Fly
WS → Pitch (forward/back)
AD → Roll (left/right)
↑↓ → Throttle (altitude)
←→ → Yaw (rotate)
Hold key = full deflection | Release = center

3. Land
Press Land button → gradual descent.

⚠ EMERGENCY
Press Backspace or Stop button.
Motors cut immediately — drone falls.
